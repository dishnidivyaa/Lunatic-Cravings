Welcome to Lunatic Cravings

Delivering great food for more than 18 years!

Are you tired of the ordinary, mundane, and predictable when it comes to food? Do you yearn for an adventure that takes your taste buds on a wild ride through uncharted gastronomic territories? Welcome to Lunatic Cravings, a culinary haven where we celebrate the extraordinary, the bizarre, and the audacious in the world of food!


Unleash Your Palate on a Journey of Unconventional Delights

Lunatic Cravings was born out of a shared passion for pushing the boundaries of culinary experiences. We are a group of daring food enthusiasts, chefs, and explorers who have embarked on a mission to unearth the most outlandish and indulgent flavors from around the globe. Our motto is to challenge conventions and redefine what food can be.

Dare to Try our Lunatic Challenges:

If you're an adventurous soul, gear up for our legendary "Lunatic Challenges." These are not for the faint-hearted but for those seeking bragging rights and an unparalleled gastronomic thrill. "Inferno Burger Challenge" - Face the fiery wrath of the Inferno Burger, laden with the hottest chili peppers known to man. Finish it within the time limit, and eternal glory awaits! "Phantom Feast" - Step into the realm of mystery with our blindfolded tasting menu, where your senses will be put to the test as you identify unique flavors and textures without sight.

Mushroom Medley with Saffron-infused Rice

Embark on an enchanting culinary journey with our Mushroom Medley, artfully paired with saffron-infused rice, at Lunatic Cravings. This exquisite vegetarian dish celebrates the earthy essence of mushrooms, enhanced by the delicate aroma of saffron, creating a symphony of flavors that will transport you to a world of culinary magic.

~ A Vegetarian Delight

Fruit Symphony

Indulge in the refreshing and vibrant flavors of our Exquisite Fruit Symphony at Lunatic Cravings. This delightful fruit salad is a harmonious medley of the finest seasonal fruits, meticulously selected for their juiciness and sweetness. Each bite is a celebration of nature's bounty, a burst of colors and flavors that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you craving for more.

~ A Symphony of Freshness and Sweetness

Savory Chinese Soupy Noodles

Indulge in the heartwarming and flavorful Chinese Soupy Noodles at Lunatic Cravings. This delightful dish combines the goodness of silky noodles, a savory broth, and a medley of fresh vegetables and tender proteins. It's a culinary delight that will transport you to the bustling streets of China, where the aroma of comforting noodle soups fills the air.

~ A Bowl of Comfort and Delight

Festive Creamy Pasta with Cherries

Celebrate the magic of Christmas with our Festive Creamy Pasta with Cherries at Lunatic Cravings. This enchanting dish combines the richness of creamy pasta with the sweetness of juicy cherries, creating a delightful fusion of flavors that embodies the spirit of the holiday season. A perfect blend of comfort and festivity, this dish will make your Christmas celebrations truly memorable.

~ A Merry Delight for Christmas

Tropical Fusion Salad

Delight in the vibrant flavors of our Exotic Fruity Medley - Blueberry, Banana, and Mango Salad with a Nutty Twist at Lunatic Cravings. This enticing salad showcases the natural sweetness of ripe mangoes, the antioxidant-packed goodness of blueberries, and the creamy texture of bananas. To add a nutty twist, we sprinkle toasted sesame seeds, elevating this salad into a culinary adventure that will transport you to a tropical paradise.

~ Exotic Tropical Fruity Medley Salad made from Blueberry, Banana, and Mango Salad with a Nutty Twist
